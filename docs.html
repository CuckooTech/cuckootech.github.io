Instructions for use

1. Open the applicationï¼Œyou will go firstly to the settings page.

2. You will get an ID consisting of 56 random characters automatically.

3. Please set your channel name & type, then descript it.

4. Save Settings.

5. By sharing the code, you can share your channel with your friends, and you can also get your favorite videos from others.

6. Below the video, you can find the "Subscribe" button. After clicking, you can receive all the updated contents of the channel. When you are no longer interested, click again to cancel the subscription.

7. Upload the video you want to share.

8. In Windows system, you can open Cuckoo repeatedly so that you can load the video you want to see at the same time.

Cuckoo
Cuckoo is a decentralized video player based on P2P connection.Cuckoo is free and always will be.Anyone or group can search, make, share and watch your favorite videos in Cuckoo without any limits or registration.
